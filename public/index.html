How Engagements Work

A structured path from first conversation to a live, performing chatbot.

Every engagement starts with understanding your business context — not just your tech stack. The goal is a chatbot that genuinely performs commercially, not just one that exists on the page. With a background spanning enterprise delivery, CTO roles, and pre-sales engineering, I connect chatbot decisions to business outcomes — and stay accountable for results.

01

Discovery

A focused session to map your audience, goals, and key conversation flows. No charge, no obligation. Honest advice about what a chatbot can and can't do for you.

02

Design & Build

We configure Botpress, design the flows, train the knowledge base on your content, and wire up your integrations. Built to your brand, tested against real scenarios.

03

Test & Refine

Two rounds of testing with your team. We handle edge cases, tune responses, fix gaps, and polish the experience before your visitors see it.

04

Live & Managed

Deployment, ongoing monitoring, monthly conversation reviews, and continuous improvement. Your team owns the outcome — not a support ticket backlog.
